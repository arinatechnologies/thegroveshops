Description

Fat Tuesday’s beginning can be traced back to Bourbon Street, New Orleans – an all-time party street in one of the world-famous towns synonymous with good times. From here, they figured out a way to prepare and sell a range of premium frozen drinks in a quicker and better fashion that shook up the business and always delivered the goods. Now, Fat Tuesday has grown to over 90 locations in warm geographies, tourist destinations, entertainment districts, and outdoor malls across the U.S and abroad. Whatever your plans and whoever you are, Fat Tuesday is here to serve up the good vibes.
